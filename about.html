What We Believe

SALVATION

Salvation in Christ has the power to transform
individuals and communities, including the black
community, by offering hope, redemption, and a path
towards a better future. In the face of challenges
such as drug abuse and violence that disproportionately
affect marginalized communities, the message of salvation
in Christ can bring about profound change and healing.

GRACE

"Grace is the gentle touch of kindness, the gift
of forgiveness, and the beauty of understanding. May we all
embrace grace in our hearts and share it with others."

GOD IN PERSON

"The black community, like many others, has faced
systemic injustices and inequalities that have contributed
to the prevalence of issues such as drug abuse and violence.
However, through the transformative power of salvation
in Christ, individuals can rise above their circumstances
and create a better future for themselves and their community.

LIFE GROUP

The teachings of Christ emphasize love, forgiveness, and compassion, which can serve as powerful antidotes to the cycles of violence and addiction that plague many communities. By embracing these principles and accepting salvation through Christ, individuals can find the strength and support needed to break free from destructive behaviors and patterns.
