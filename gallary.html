Our Activities

SUNDAY: 6:45AM

Every Sunday morning, the church welcomes
believers to refuel their faith, rejuvenate
their spirits, and reconnect with their community.
This sacred ritual offers a much-needed pause from
the hustle and bustle of daily life, providing an
opportunity to recharge and refocus.

MONDAY: 6:00PM

Beginning the week with spiritual reflection
and community connection, the church holds
services every Monday. This practice sets a
tone of hope, renewal, and purpose, equipping
believers to face the challenges ahead.

SUNDAY: 6:45AM

Church empowerment services play
a vital role in uplifting and transforming
the lives of individuals and communities.
These services go beyond traditional worship,
focusing on equipping members with skills,
knowledge, and resources to overcome challenges
and reach their full potential.
